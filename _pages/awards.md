## 4.2 Grants 

### GoviLab AgriTech Accelerator, 2024
Received grant support for Farm’s Tech (Pvt) Ltd under the GoviLab AgriTech Accelerator in 2024. The programme, implemented in partnership with Hatch and Sarvodaya under Sri Lanka’s Inclusive Digital Agriculture Transformation (IDAT) strategy, with support from the Government of Sri Lanka and the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, supports agri-tech ventures developing scalable, technology-driven solutions for the agricultural sector.

### SLIIT Research Grants, 2023
Received under the annual research grant programme of the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology for the project “Development of a Novel Detection System to Address Human-Elephant Conflict and Support the Conservation of Sri Lankan Elephants.” This grant supported research conducted under Dr. Janaka Wijekoon, who served as Principal Investigator.
